# QR Bill - Simple Billing & Payments

A clean, simple tool that helps you create bills and get paid instantly using QR codes.

---

## What It Does

**QR Bill** makes billing easy. Fill in a few details, and it:

- Creates a professional bill receipt
- Generates a QR code your customer can scan to pay
- Automatically sends the bill to your customer via WhatsApp
- Lets you print a paper copy if needed

No complicated setup. No sign-up required. Just open and start billing.

---

## How to Use

1. **Open the app** — Double-click the `index.html` file, or open it in any web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
2. **Fill in the details:**
   - **Item Name** — What was sold (e.g., "Rice 5kg", "Shirt")
   - **Amount** — Price in Indian Rupees (₹)
   - **Customer Phone** — Their 10-digit mobile number
   - **Customer Name** — Optional, but helps you remember who bought what
3. **Click "Generate Bill"** — The app will:
   - Show a QR code on screen
   - Open WhatsApp with a ready-to-send message
   - Show you a printable bill receipt
4. **Send the message** — Just press Enter in WhatsApp to send the bill to your customer
5. **Print if needed** — Click "Print Bill" to get a paper copy

---

## Features

- **QR Code Payments** — Your customer scans the QR code and pays directly using any UPI app (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, etc.)
- **WhatsApp Integration** — The bill is automatically sent to your customer's phone
- **Printable Receipts** — Get a clean, professional receipt you can print
- **Works on Any Device** — Use it on your phone, tablet, or computer
- **No Internet Needed** — Works offline once the page is loaded
- **No Registration** — Just open the file and start using it

---

## Requirements

- A modern web browser (Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, or Safari)
- An internet connection (only needed the first time to load the page, and to open WhatsApp)

That's it. Nothing else to install.

## Tips

- The QR code uses **UPI** (Unified Payments Interface), so your customer needs a UPI-enabled app to scan and pay
- You can use this tool for any kind of billing — shops, services, freelancing, events, etc.
- The bill receipt shows all the important details: date, time, item, amount, and a QR code for payment

---

## Need Help?

If something isn't working:
1. Make sure you're using a modern browser (Chrome is recommended)
2. Check that your internet connection is working
3. Make sure the customer's phone number is exactly 10 digits
